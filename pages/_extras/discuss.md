## Getting Help

### During Training
- Ask questions in the Zoom chat
- Use the "Raise Hand" feature for live questions
- Facilitators will monitor the chat and address questions

### After Training
- **Matrix**: Join NRP's Matrix for community support - [https://nrp.ai/contact/](https://nrp.ai/contact/)
- **Email**: Contact usersupport@nrp-nautilus.io for technical support
- **Documentation**: Check the [NRP Documentation](https://nrp.ai/documentation/) for detailed guides

## Community Resources

- **NRP Portal**: [https://nrp.ai](https://nrp.ai) - Main entry point for NRP resources
- **Namespace Portal**: [https://nrp.ai/viz/namespaces/](https://nrp.ai/viz/namespaces/) - View and manage namespaces
- **Office Hours**: NRP hosts bi-weekly office hours - check the portal for schedule

## Training Feedback

We welcome feedback on the training materials and sessions. Please reach out via:
- Matrix: Contact the facilitators directly
- Email: usersupport@nrp-nautilus.io
